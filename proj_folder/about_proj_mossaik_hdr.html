Mossaik HDR

Technologies
CoreML, OpenCV, OpenCL, Metal, Accelerate

A perfect mix of technology and art, Mossaik HDR offers a unique photo editing experience, especially for HDR space editing. With feature like HDR merge and AI HDR create, Mossaik HDR is very unique in the market space.

Optimised for AMD GPUs and using RDNA, Mossaik HDR has Core ML, Open CV, Open CL, Metal, Accelerate, running in the background, offering all edits in realtime.

With unlimited history and Non Destructive Editing (NDe), Mossaik HDR provides full flexibility with no restrictions on creators imagination.

The editor runs on all Mac computers released after 2013.
