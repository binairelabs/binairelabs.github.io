Spectrum HDR
Designed specifically for Windows platform, Spectrum HDR uses the best of VULKAN, CUDA, Open CV which form the backbone of the graphic processor and renderer.
Technologies
Vulkan, CUDA, OpenCV

Designed specifically for Windows platform, Spectrum HDR uses the best of VULKAN, CUDA, Open CV which form the backbone of the graphic processor and renderer.

Spectrum HDR offers HDR editing in multiple ICC profiles. Supporting RAW image and common compressed image formats, the software engages the GPU and offers any frame rate unto 240 Hz.

With over 50 local and global adjustments, open & edit images in numerous image formats like JPEG, TIFF, PNG, HEIF, BMP, JPEG-2000 and RAW formats. Export images as JPG, PNG, TIFF and BMP.
