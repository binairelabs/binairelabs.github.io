ONYX
ONYX is a contemporary Visual Audio Mixer designed to develop music and synths across all major platforms, including the web. Create, edit and export with support for all major audio codecs, or share the complete project online.
Technologies
Web, C++, WebAssembly, GPU Audio, Web Audio

ONYX is a contemporary Visual Audio Mixer designed to develop music and synths across all major platforms, including the web. Create, edit and export with support for all major audio codecs, or share the complete project online (the application is supported currently on CHROME and FIREFOX).

The unique aspect of ONYX is the creative use of GPU to synthesize audio, providing lightening fast and unlimited range of all elements of audio, even beyond human perception of audio frequencies.

Purest and cleanest possible notes, ONYX is a perfect platform to create music from absolute scratch with LEGO style building blocks.
